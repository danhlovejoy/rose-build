What's in This Module

  1. Portfolio Prep Guide: How to choose your projects, structure your narrative, build your visual aids, and practice your delivery. Includes a rubric walkthrough so you know exactly what's being evaluated.
  2. Combined Portfolio: Multimodal Portfolio. For dual-enrolled students. One presentation covering both courses, including a synthesis project that combines NLP and CV.
  3. Assignment Sheet: Portfolio presentation details, final reflection details, and due dates.
  4. Ethics Discussion: Final capstone ethics prompt reflecting on the semester.
Tip: The portfolio presentation is 10% of your grade — the single largest individual assignment. Don't treat it as an afterthought. The students who do well are the ones who rehearse at least twice, time themselves, and cut material until they're comfortably under seven minutes. Thursday lab is your best rehearsal opportunity.
