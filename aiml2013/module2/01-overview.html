Module 2: Hand-Crafted Features (HOG)

AIML 2013 • Introduction to Computer Vision • Spring 2026

Before deep learning, computer vision ran on features that researchers designed by hand. This week you'll compute HOG descriptors, use them to retrieve visually similar images, and find out exactly where the approach works and where it falls apart.

This Week's Focus

Extract HOG descriptors from images, build a similarity retrieval system using cosine similarity, and systematically identify what HOG captures and what it throws away.
