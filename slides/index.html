Inside the Pipeline: TF-IDF + Logistic Regression

Trace a movie review through every step — tokenization, TF-IDF vectorization, the dot product, and sigmoid classification. Type your own text to see how the model processes it.

 Logistic Regression: The Binary Classifier

Drag a decision boundary, watch gradient descent train in real time, inspect individual predictions, and see where the model fails. A two-feature visualization of how binary classification works.

 Cosine Similarity: Measuring How Alike Two Things Are

Drag 2D vectors to build geometric intuition, compare documents by their words, compare images by their edges, and explore similarity matrices. The same math serves both NLP and CV.

 Pixel Histograms: How Machines See Light

Load an image, inspect individual pixel values, and watch brightness histograms update in real time. See how grayscale conversion, thresholding, and equalization change what the machine sees.
